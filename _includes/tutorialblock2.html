## Troubleshooting *My headphones aren’t appearing in the Logitech HUB.* - The computer has not registered the headphones. Remove the USB and re-insert it. Alternatively, remove the USB, restart your computer, and then re-insert the USB. *The Logitech HUB says my headphones are inactive.* - Switch the power button on the headphones off and then on again. Alternatively, open and close the headphone’s microphone arm. *My Speaker Setup window only has a Stereo option, not a 7.1 Surround option.* - Your headphones have not been configured in the Logitech G HUB. Download the HUB and configure the headphones in there first. See the beginning of this tutorial for directions.
